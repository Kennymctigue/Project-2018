## Backround to the Iris Flower Dataset

[The Iris Flower Dataset](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iris_flower_data_set) is also known as [Fisher's](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ronald_Fisher) Iris data set, after the British statistician and Biologist Ronald Fisher.

The dataset was compiled by taking 50 samples each of three different species of Iris flower, Iris Setosa, Iris Virginica and Iris Versicolor.  For each sample taken four different measurements were recorded, the sepal length, the sepal width, the petal length and the petal width
